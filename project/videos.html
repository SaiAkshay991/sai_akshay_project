Topic 1 ADDITION

Addition means altogether, counting of two groups of objects and finding how many in all. In adding numbers basic skills required to succeed counting up to 10.

Topic 2 SUBTRACTION

In math, to subtract means to take away from a group or a number of things. When we subtract, the number of things in the group reduce or become less.

Topic 3 MULTIPLICATION

Just like addition, multiplication is commutative, meaning the order of factors doesn't change the product (the answer). In other words, two numbers can be multiplied in any order, and the product will be the same.

Topic 4 DIVISION

The division is a method of distributing a group of things into equal parts. It is one of the four basic operations of arithmetic, which gives a fair result of sharing. The division is an operation inverse of multiplication.
